LIFECHECK

Addressing the lack of quick triage tools for Community Health Workers in Northern Province's rural health posts.

Our Team Problem Solution Team

Our Team

We are a group of dedicated professionals committed to improving rural healthcare in Northern Province.

Problem

Rural health posts in Northern Province lack a structured triage system, forcing Community Health Workers to rely on judgment without standardized guidance. Triage is meant to determine what help a patient needs, how urgent it is, and who should provide it, but this process is currently inconsistent. As a result, patients may be delayed, misdirected, or receive inappropriate care, especially when many cases arrive at once and priorities are unclear.

Solution

LifeCheck introduces a simple digital triage tool that guides Community Health Workers through structured questions to assess symptoms, urgency, and appropriate care pathways. By capturing patient information and prioritizing cases based on need, the system ensures that critical cases are identified quickly and directed to the right level of care. This mirrors modern triage systems that sort patients efficiently and help providers deliver faster, more appropriate treatment decisions.
